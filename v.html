Volunteer work

Boys2men-Girls2women is a community non-profit organization that is committed to serving the community by assisting children between the ages of 6-18 years of age in becoming productive members of society by instilling positive, spiritual, cultural, educational, vocational, and social values also, we are doing something about Fresno’s alarming school dropout rate which includes the teen pregnancy rate

Mission Statement.

Our mission is to make a positive difference in a child’s life by offering encouragement and support. We have mentors and tutors who are skilled and gracefully willing to give time and effort to assist a child in improving in their future in life and making the grade, we make sure this is done with efficiency and confidence. We wish this to be a rewarding experience and hope that each child gains a wonderful understanding of what it is like to have a positive role model for the future generation.
